About Us

In a world where trade and communication is heavily globalised and competition is fierce, value is derived from four key facets: efficiency, effectiveness, transparency and trust.​ Comdex is the first blockchain based platform for seamless and transparent settlement of cross border trade settlements



Comdex is the first cross-border commodity trade settlement platform built on the blockchain. It aims to revolutionize the rudimentary processes of global commodity trade by minimizing settlement time while encouraging maximum transparency and immutability in trade discovery and trade financing.​​Using a proprietary settlement platform built on the tenets of Tendermint and Inter-blockchain communication, Comdex proposes to utilize the potential of a custom stablecoin (cUSD). This will reduce the need for dependence on traditional banking escrow systems that usually have high costs along with complex validation procedures.


With rapid expansion in the global economy, the availability of key commodities plays a pivotal role in maintaining the pace of growth.Traditional economics has always pushed us towards reducing uncertainties in trade. However, over time, these practices have seen a gradual transition into an ecosystem of high cost structures and lengthy processes.

In a world of growing interconnectedness, commodity trade finds itself left behind due to a lack of trust amongst the players in the sector. This has created a vacuum that banks and other institutional benefactors have filled. However, there is now an opportunity for blockchain technology to address these discrepancies and gain widespread adoption.



“The commodity trade industry is undoubtedly the lifeblood of the global supply chain and it must evolve along with technology in order to combat the threats of the future. Comdex will be one of the first solutions that transforms this industry into one that is seamless and effective”
Uday Joshi
CEO, Comdex
