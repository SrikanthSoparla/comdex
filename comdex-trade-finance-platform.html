Comdex's Trade Finance Platform

The Comdex trade finance platform allows financial organizations to finance open trades that will be settled on the Comdex platform. The transparency of the platform coupled with with increased trade visibility helps traders establish credibility and access capital.


The availability of financing for cross-border trades is imperative for traders to meet the burgeoning requirements of the rapidly growing economies. Simply put, the commodity trade industry would not function without the presence of the trade finance industry. Any funding request of a considerable amount requires financers to perform thorough due-diligence to assess the potential risk of the investment. The ability for trade financers to conduct due-diligence is affected by the lack of a properly quantified and consolidated credit-score. Existing rating platforms like D&B DUNS are limited by subjectivity in their ratings. ​

Deal Flow Visibility

Financers have access to viewing requests for trade financing from commodity traders present on the Comdex platform. Increased visibility of trustworthy requests will result in a growth in lending volume for trade financers in the longer run, significantly making an impact on long-term profitability.​

Transparency

Comdex is an end-to-end commodity trading platform which includes everything from trade-related document processing to the final settlement.​
